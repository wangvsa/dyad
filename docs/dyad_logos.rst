DYAD Logo Reference
===================

All logo variants for the DYAD brand. Files are located in ``_static/logo/``.

Rows 1–4 show the four colorways (Standard and Streamliner editions, light and dark backgrounds).
Row 5 shows the monochrome white (dark background) and black (light background) transparency-mask variants.
